<h1>Term Glossary</h1>
<p>The Glossary lets you define the key terms and acronyms used in your documentation. Once defined, terms are automatically highlighted wherever they appear in your articles, and readers can hover over them to see the definition — without leaving the page. The Glossary also generates a standalone public Glossary page on your knowledge base.</p>

<h2>Opening the Glossary</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Toolbox</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
<li>Under <strong>Content Blocks</strong>, click <strong>Term Glossary</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>Adding a Term</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>+ Add Term</strong></li>
<li>Enter the <strong>term</strong> — the word or acronym to define (e.g., "API", "Knowledge Base", "Widget")</li>
<li>Enter the <strong>definition</strong> — a clear, concise explanation (1–3 sentences is ideal)</li>
<li>Optionally add <strong>synonyms</strong> — alternative spellings or abbreviations that should also trigger the tooltip (e.g., "KB" as a synonym for "Knowledge Base")</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>How Glossary Terms Appear in Articles</h2>
<p>Once a term is defined, FinalDoc automatically scans all articles for that term and highlights it with a subtle dotted underline. When a reader hovers over the highlighted term, a tooltip appears with the definition — no navigation required, no external link to follow.</p>
<p>This works on your public knowledge base, not in the admin editor.</p>

<h2>The Public Glossary Page</h2>
<p>All defined terms are automatically collected on a public Glossary page at:</p>
<pre><code>https://your-project.finaldoc.io/glossary</code></pre>
<p>This page lists all terms alphabetically with their definitions. Readers can navigate to it from your KB sidebar (if you enable it) or link to it from articles. It is a great resource for new readers who need to get familiar with your terminology.</p>
<p>To show the Glossary link in your KB sidebar, enable it in <strong>Settings → Appearance → Portal Builder → Navigation Options</strong>.</p>

<h2>Editing and Deleting Terms</h2>
<ul>
<li>Click any term in the Glossary list to edit its definition, synonyms, or spelling</li>
<li>Click the trash icon to delete a term — the highlighting disappears from articles immediately, and the term is removed from the public Glossary page</li>
</ul>

<h2>Best Practices</h2>
<ul>
<li>Define acronyms on first use — especially industry jargon that new users might not know</li>
<li>Keep definitions concise — the tooltip is small, so long definitions get cut off</li>
<li>Add synonyms generously — readers may use different words for the same concept</li>
<li>Link the definition to a full article when more detail is needed: "An API (Application Programming Interface) is... [Learn more in the API Overview article]"</li>
</ul>
