<h1>Article Feedback &amp; Ratings</h1>
<p>The Article Feedback system lets readers tell you if your articles were helpful. A "Helpful? Yes / No" widget appears at the bottom of every published article. Readers can optionally add a written comment. All feedback is collected in the admin <strong>Feedback</strong> inbox.</p>

<h2>Enabling Article Feedback</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings &rarr; Appearance &rarr; Article Display</strong></li>
<li>Expand <strong>Article bottom</strong></li>
<li>Toggle <strong>Show feedback form</strong> ON</li>
</ol>

<h2>How Readers Submit Feedback</h2>
<ol>
<li>At the bottom of each article, readers see: <strong>"Helpful?"</strong> with <strong>Yes</strong> and <strong>No</strong> buttons</li>
<li>Clicking <strong>Yes</strong> immediately submits positive feedback</li>
<li>Clicking <strong>No</strong> reveals a text field: "What was missing or wrong?"</li>
<li>The reader can type a comment and submit, or just submit without a comment</li>
<li>A "Thanks for your feedback!" message confirms the submission</li>
</ol>

<h2>Viewing Feedback</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Feedback</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
<li>The <strong>Article Feedback</strong> tab shows all submitted feedback</li>
</ol>
<p>Each card shows the article title, rating (positive/negative), comment (if any), date, and reader name (or "Reader" for anonymous users).</p>

<h2>Action Buttons</h2>
<p>Hover over any feedback card to see action buttons:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Edit Article</strong> (pencil) &mdash; Opens the article in the Documentation editor</li>
<li><strong>View Article</strong> (external link) &mdash; Opens the article on the public KB</li>
<li><strong>Create Task</strong> (checklist) &mdash; Creates a task on the Home page. Negative feedback = HIGH priority. The button becomes disabled after a task is created to prevent duplicates</li>
<li><strong>Mark Resolved</strong> (checkmark) &mdash; Opens a dialog for an optional resolution note</li>
<li><strong>Reply</strong> (message) &mdash; Email a response to the reader (only available for logged-in readers with an email)</li>
<li><strong>Delete</strong> (trash) &mdash; Soft-delete with 5-second undo</li>
</ul>

<h2>Feedback Workflow</h2>
<ol>
<li>Review negative feedback in the Feedback inbox</li>
<li>Click <strong>Create Task</strong> to create a work item (appears as <strong>F-001</strong> in the Tasks card)</li>
<li>Go to <strong>Home &rarr; Tasks</strong> and assign the task to a writer</li>
<li>Writer edits and improves the article</li>
<li>Mark the feedback as <strong>Resolved</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>Analytics</h2>
<p>The Article Feedback tab includes three collapsible analytics sections:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>14-Day Sentiment Trend</strong> &mdash; Area chart showing positive vs negative feedback over time</li>
<li><strong>AI Insights</strong> &mdash; Priority articles, trending issues, common themes, sentiment heatmap</li>
<li><strong>Your Performance</strong> &mdash; Resolution streak, total handled, average response time, achievements</li>
</ul>
