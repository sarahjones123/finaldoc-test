<h1>Reader Management</h1>
<p>Readers are the end users of your public knowledge base. If your KB requires login (Private or Mixed visibility), you need to manage who can create an account and what access they have. This guide covers managing reader accounts.</p>

<h2>Opening Reader Management</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings → Security &amp; Access → Reader Management</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>The Reader List</h2>
<p>The reader list shows all registered readers with:</p>
<ul>
<li>Email address</li>
<li>Name (if they provided it during registration)</li>
<li>Join date</li>
<li>Last login date</li>
<li>Reader group(s) they belong to</li>
<li>Status (Active, Invited, Suspended)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Inviting Readers</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Invite Reader</strong></li>
<li>Enter their email address</li>
<li>Optionally assign them to a reader group immediately</li>
<li>Click <strong>Send Invite</strong></li>
<li>The reader receives an email invitation with a link to create their account</li>
</ol>

<h2>Self-Registration</h2>
<p>Instead of manually inviting every reader, you can allow readers to register themselves:</p>
<ol>
<li>In Reader Management, find <strong>Allow Self-Registration</strong></li>
<li>Toggle it ON</li>
<li>The KB login page shows a "Create Account" link</li>
<li>New readers fill in their email and set a password</li>
</ol>
<p>Self-registered readers get basic access. Assign them to groups manually or use automatic group assignment rules (Enterprise feature).</p>

<h2>Removing a Reader</h2>
<ol>
<li>Find the reader in the list</li>
<li>Click the three-dot menu → <strong>Remove</strong></li>
<li>The reader's account is deactivated immediately. Their session ends and they cannot log in again</li>
</ol>

<h2>Resetting a Reader's Password</h2>
<ol>
<li>Find the reader in the list</li>
<li>Click the three-dot menu → <strong>Reset Password</strong></li>
<li>The reader receives an email with a password reset link</li>
</ol>

<h2>Reader Profile</h2>
<p>Readers can update their own profile (name, password) from the KB — there is a profile link in the KB header when they are logged in. They cannot change their email address (contact admin for that).</p>

<h2>Bulk Actions</h2>
<p>Select multiple readers and use bulk actions to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Add to a group</li>
<li>Remove from a group</li>
<li>Send bulk password reset email</li>
<li>Remove multiple readers at once</li>
</ul>
