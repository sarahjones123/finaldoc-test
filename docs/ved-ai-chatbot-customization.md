<h1>Ved AI Chatbot Customization</h1>
<p>The Ved AI chatbot can be extensively customized to match your brand and serve your readers better. You can change its name, appearance, personality, greeting messages, suggested questions, and fallback behavior. All customization is done in the AI Configuration settings.</p>

<h2>Opening Chatbot Customization</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings → AI (Ved AI) → AI Configuration</strong></li>
<li>Scroll to the <strong>Chatbot Appearance &amp; Behavior</strong> section</li>
</ol>

<h2>Chatbot Identity</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Chatbot Name</strong> — What the bot calls itself (e.g., "Alex", "Aria", "Support Bot", "DocBot"). Shown in the chat header and in "Hi, I'm [name]" greetings</li>
<li><strong>Avatar</strong> — Upload a custom avatar image for the chatbot. PNG/SVG, shown in a circle. Use your company mascot, a generic robot icon, or a professional abstract icon</li>
<li><strong>Accent Color</strong> — The chatbot's message bubble color. Defaults to your brand primary color</li>
</ul>

<h2>Greeting Message</h2>
<p>The first message the chatbot sends when a reader opens the chat:</p>
<ul>
<li>Keep it friendly and brief</li>
<li>Tell readers what the bot can help with</li>
<li>Example: "Hi! I'm Alex, your [Product] assistant. Ask me anything about using [Product] and I'll find the answer from our documentation."</li>
</ul>

<h2>Suggested Questions</h2>
<p>Quick-reply buttons shown at the start of the chat conversation. These prompt readers to click a common question instead of typing from scratch:</p>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>+ Add Suggested Question</strong></li>
<li>Type the question text (e.g., "How do I reset my password?")</li>
<li>Add up to 4 suggested questions</li>
</ol>
<p>Good suggested questions are the most common first questions new readers ask. Check your search analytics for inspiration.</p>

<h2>Fallback Message</h2>
<p>What the chatbot says when it can't find an answer in your KB:</p>
<ul>
<li>Default: "I'm sorry, I couldn't find a good answer to that question in our documentation."</li>
<li>Customize to offer alternatives: "I couldn't find an answer for that. You can contact our team at support@yourcompany.com or use the contact form below."</li>
</ul>

<h2>Chatbot Button Style</h2>
<p>Configure the button that opens the chatbot on your KB:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Classic</strong> — A circular floating button with a chat icon</li>
<li><strong>Capsule</strong> — A pill-shaped button showing both Voice and Chat options side by side (the Ved AI capsule button)</li>
<li><strong>Inline</strong> — A chat input directly embedded in the KB homepage (not a floating button)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Proactive Messages</h2>
<p>Enable proactive messages to have the chatbot send an unprompted message after a reader has been on an article for a set time:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Trigger delay</strong> — How many seconds before the proactive message appears (e.g., 30 seconds)</li>
<li><strong>Message</strong> — What the chatbot says proactively (e.g., "Need help understanding this article? Ask me!")</li>
<li><strong>Show only on specific articles</strong> — Target proactive messages to specific articles or categories</li>
</ul>
<p>Use proactive messages sparingly — they can be intrusive if overused.</p>
