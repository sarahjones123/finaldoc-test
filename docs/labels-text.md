<h1>Labels &amp; Text</h1>
<p>The Labels &amp; Text settings let you customize the text strings that appear throughout your public knowledge base interface — button labels, placeholder text, section headings, error messages, and more. Use this to match your brand voice, translate to a different language, or personalize the reader experience.</p>

<h2>Opening Labels &amp; Text</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings → Appearance → Labels &amp; Text</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>What Can Be Customized</h2>
<p>Labels &amp; Text covers the UI copy for your public KB — not article content. Examples of strings you can customize:</p>
<table>
<thead><tr><th>Default Text</th><th>Example Customization</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td>"Search..." (placeholder in search bar)</td><td>"Search 200+ articles..."</td></tr>
<tr><td>"Was this article helpful?"</td><td>"Did this answer your question?"</td></tr>
<tr><td>"Yes" / "No" (feedback buttons)</td><td>"Helpful" / "Not helpful"</td></tr>
<tr><td>"No results found"</td><td>"We couldn't find anything. Try different keywords."</td></tr>
<tr><td>"Ask AI" (chatbot button)</td><td>"Chat with Aria"</td></tr>
<tr><td>"Contact Us" (contact form trigger)</td><td>"Talk to our team"</td></tr>
<tr><td>"Category" (navigation label)</td><td>"Section"</td></tr>
<tr><td>"Updated" (last updated label)</td><td>"Last revised"</td></tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<h2>Changing a Label</h2>
<ol>
<li>Find the label you want to change in the list</li>
<li>Click on the text field next to it</li>
<li>Type your custom text</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save</strong> or press Tab to move to the next field (changes auto-save)</li>
</ol>

<h2>Resetting to Default</h2>
<p>Click the <strong>Reset</strong> button (circular arrow) next to any customized label to revert it to the FinalDoc default. Click <strong>Reset All</strong> at the top to reset every label to defaults at once.</p>

<h2>Language / Localization</h2>
<p>Labels &amp; Text is the simplest way to translate your KB interface into another language without a full localization setup. For example, if your KB is French-language, translate all the UI strings to French here — search placeholder becomes "Rechercher...", feedback question becomes "Cet article vous a-t-il aidé?", etc.</p>
<p>For full multi-language support with multiple language versions of articles, see the <strong>Localization &amp; Multi-Language Setup</strong> article.</p>

<h2>Changes Are Live Immediately</h2>
<p>Label changes take effect on your public KB immediately after saving — no rebuild or deployment needed.</p>
