<h1>Migrate Content</h1>
<p>The Migrate Content tool in the Toolbox helps you move articles between workspaces, reorganize content at scale, and merge or split documentation sections without having to manually copy-paste each article.</p>

<h2>Opening Migrate Content</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Toolbox</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
<li>Click <strong>Migrate Content</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>What You Can Migrate</h2>

<h3>Move Articles Between Workspaces</h3>
<p>If you have multiple workspaces (e.g., v1 and v2 documentation), you can move articles from one workspace to another:</p>
<ol>
<li>In Migrate Content, select <strong>Move Articles Between Workspaces</strong></li>
<li>Choose the <strong>source workspace</strong></li>
<li>Select the articles to move (or select entire categories)</li>
<li>Choose the <strong>destination workspace</strong></li>
<li>Choose the destination category (or create a new one)</li>
<li>Click <strong>Move</strong></li>
</ol>

<h3>Copy Articles to Another Workspace</h3>
<p>Same as Move, but leaves the original article intact and creates a copy in the destination. Useful for creating localized versions of articles.</p>

<h3>Merge Categories</h3>
<ol>
<li>Select <strong>Merge Categories</strong></li>
<li>Select two or more source categories</li>
<li>Select (or create) the destination category</li>
<li>All articles from source categories move into the destination</li>
<li>Source categories are deleted (empty after migration)</li>
</ol>

<h3>Reorganize via CSV</h3>
<p>For large-scale restructuring:</p>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Export Structure CSV</strong> — downloads a spreadsheet of all articles with their current categories</li>
<li>Edit the CSV in Excel or Sheets — change category names, reorder, restructure as needed</li>
<li>Upload the modified CSV</li>
<li>FinalDoc applies your new structure</li>
</ol>
<p>This is the fastest way to reorganize dozens or hundreds of articles — much faster than drag-and-drop in the sidebar.</p>

<h2>Bulk Status Change</h2>
<p>Change the status of many articles at once:</p>
<ol>
<li>In Migrate Content, select <strong>Bulk Status Change</strong></li>
<li>Filter articles by current status, category, or date</li>
<li>Select the articles to change</li>
<li>Set the new status (Draft → Published, Published → Archived, etc.)</li>
<li>Click <strong>Apply</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>Safety</h2>
<p>All Migrate Content operations create an automatic backup before executing. If something goes wrong, you can restore the pre-migration state from Settings → Backup &amp; Restore.</p>
