<h1>Pinning and Featuring Articles</h1>
<p>FinalDoc gives you two ways to make important articles stand out: <strong>pinning</strong> keeps an article at the top of its category, and the <strong>featured article</strong> setting highlights it prominently on the knowledge base homepage.</p>

<h2>Pinning an Article</h2>
<p>A pinned article always appears at the top of its category in the public knowledge base sidebar, regardless of the normal sort order. Use pinning for your most important or frequently accessed articles — like "Getting Started", "Installation Guide", or "FAQ".</p>

<h3>How to Pin</h3>
<ol>
<li>Open the article in the editor</li>
<li>In the right <strong>Inspector panel</strong>, look for the <strong>Pin Article</strong> toggle</li>
<li>Enable the toggle — the article is now pinned</li>
<li>In the sidebar, pinned articles display a small pin icon next to their name</li>
</ol>
<p>You can pin multiple articles in the same category. Pinned articles appear in the order they were pinned (most recently pinned last). You can drag pinned articles to change their order relative to each other.</p>

<h3>How to Unpin</h3>
<ol>
<li>Open the pinned article</li>
<li>Find the <strong>Pin Article</strong> toggle in the Inspector panel</li>
<li>Disable the toggle</li>
<li>The article returns to its normal sort position</li>
</ol>

<h2>Featured Articles</h2>
<p>A featured article is highlighted on your knowledge base homepage in a prominent card or banner. It is a great way to draw attention to a new guide, a popular article, or a critical notice.</p>

<h3>How to Feature an Article</h3>
<ol>
<li>Open the article in the editor</li>
<li>In the Inspector panel, find the <strong>Feature this Article</strong> or <strong>Featured Image</strong> toggle</li>
<li>Enable it and optionally set a <strong>featured image</strong> — this image appears in the featured card on the homepage</li>
<li>The article appears in the "Featured" section of your public KB homepage</li>
</ol>

<h2>Article Sort Order</h2>
<p>By default, articles within a category are displayed in the order you have arranged them (drag-and-drop in the admin sidebar). The sort order of your admin view is the exact order readers see. To change the display order:</p>
<ol>
<li>In the Documentation view, find the article in the left panel</li>
<li>Grab the drag handle (six dots on the left of the article name)</li>
<li>Drag it up or down to the desired position</li>
<li>Release — the new order is saved immediately and visible to readers</li>
</ol>

<h2>Smart Bars and Homepage Spotlights</h2>
<p>Beyond pinning and featuring, you can also use <strong>Smart Bars</strong> (Settings → Appearance → Smart Bars) to display a prominent announcement bar at the top of your knowledge base. Smart Bars are perfect for time-sensitive alerts like maintenance windows, new feature announcements, or links to important resources.</p>
