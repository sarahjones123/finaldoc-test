<h1>Smart Bars</h1>
<p>Smart bars are custom announcement banners that appear on your public knowledge base. Use them to notify readers about maintenance windows, new features, promotions, important alerts, or any time-sensitive information. Smart bars are fully customizable — you control the message, colors, position, and schedule.</p>

<h2>Opening Smart Bars</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings → Appearance → Smart Bars</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>Creating a Smart Bar</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>New Smart Bar</strong></li>
<li>A configuration panel slides in from the right</li>
<li>Fill in the details (see below)</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>Smart Bar Settings</h2>

<h3>Name</h3>
<p>An internal name for your own reference — readers don't see this. Example: "Holiday maintenance notice" or "Product launch banner".</p>

<h3>Active Toggle</h3>
<p>Toggle next to the Name field. When active (green), the bar is visible on your KB. Turn it off to hide the bar without deleting it.</p>

<h3>Content</h3>
<p>The message shown in the bar. You can use the rich text editor to format the text — bold, italic, links, and more. Keep it short — one or two sentences works best. Example: "We'll be doing maintenance on March 15 from 2–4 AM UTC. The site will be temporarily unavailable."</p>

<h3>Global</h3>
<p>When toggled on, the smart bar appears on every page of your KB. When toggled off, you can specify which pages it appears on using conditions (see below).</p>

<h2>Design and Location</h2>

<h3>Location</h3>
<p>Choose where the bar appears on the page:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Top bar</strong> — A horizontal strip at the very top of the page, above the KB header (most prominent)</li>
<li><strong>Bottom bar</strong> — A strip fixed to the bottom of the browser window</li>
<li><strong>Floating</strong> — A floating notification-style banner (typically bottom-right)</li>
</ul>

<h3>Bar Style</h3>
<p>Choose the visual theme of the bar:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Light theme</strong> — White/light gray background with dark text</li>
<li><strong>Dark theme</strong> — Dark background with light text</li>
<li><strong>Custom theme</strong> — Pick your own colors for background, text, link color, and border</li>
</ul>

<h3>Custom Colors (when Custom Theme is selected)</h3>
<p>Set exact hex colors for:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Background</strong> — The main bar background color</li>
<li><strong>Text</strong> — The message text color</li>
<li><strong>Link Color</strong> — Color of any links inside the bar message</li>
<li><strong>Border</strong> — The bar's border color (subtle accent at the bottom or edge)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Scheduling</h2>
<p>Enable the schedule toggle to set a start and end date for the smart bar to be automatically shown and hidden:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Start Date</strong> — The bar becomes visible on this date</li>
<li><strong>End Date</strong> — The bar automatically hides after this date</li>
</ul>
<p>This is perfect for time-sensitive announcements like promotions or planned outages — set it and forget it.</p>

<h2>Show Conditions</h2>
<p>If <strong>Global</strong> is turned off, you can set conditions for when the bar appears:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Specific articles</strong> — Show only on certain articles (by slug or URL match)</li>
<li><strong>Specific categories</strong> — Show only when viewing articles in a certain category</li>
<li><strong>URL pattern</strong> — Show when the URL matches a pattern (e.g., contains "/api-docs")</li>
</ul>

<h2>Managing Smart Bars</h2>
<p>All smart bars appear in a list on the Smart Bars settings page. Each one shows:</p>
<ul>
<li>Name</li>
<li>Active/Inactive status badge</li>
<li>Content preview</li>
<li>Location and style</li>
<li>A toggle to quickly activate/deactivate</li>
<li>A delete button (X)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Use Case Examples</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Planned maintenance</strong>: "We'll be offline for maintenance on Saturday March 15 from 2–4 AM UTC."</li>
<li><strong>New feature announcement</strong>: "We just launched Video Studio! <a href='#'>Check it out →</a>"</li>
<li><strong>Free trial promo</strong>: "Start your free 14-day trial — no credit card required. <a href='#'>Sign up now</a>"</li>
<li><strong>Version deprecation notice</strong>: "API v1 is being deprecated on April 1. Please migrate to v2."</li>
<li><strong>Holiday hours</strong>: "Our support team will be out December 25–26. Responses may be delayed."</li>
</ul>
