<h1>Contacting Support</h1>
<p>If you can't find the answer you need in the FinalDoc documentation, our support team is here to help. This article explains how to reach us and what to include in your request for the fastest resolution.</p>

<h2>Support Channels</h2>

<h3>Email Support</h3>
<p>Send your question to <strong>support@finaldoc.io</strong>.</p>
<p>Response times:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Free plan</strong> — 3–5 business days</li>
<li><strong>Starter plan</strong> — 2 business days</li>
<li><strong>Business plan</strong> — 1 business day</li>
<li><strong>Enterprise plan</strong> — Same day (Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm UTC)</li>
</ul>

<h3>In-App Chat</h3>
<p>Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner of the FinalDoc admin panel to start a live chat with the support team. Chat availability depends on your plan and current agent availability.</p>

<h3>Documentation (You're Here)</h3>
<p>Search for your question using the search bar at the top of this page. Over 180 articles cover every FinalDoc feature in detail — most common questions are answered here.</p>

<h3>Ved AI Chatbot</h3>
<p>The AI assistant on this documentation site (the chat button in the corner) can answer questions about FinalDoc features instantly using this knowledge base. Ask it anything — it's available 24/7.</p>

<h2>What to Include in Your Support Request</h2>
<p>For the fastest resolution, include these details when emailing support:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Project name and URL</strong> — e.g., "My project is 'Acme Help Center' at acme-help.finaldoc.io"</li>
<li><strong>What you're trying to do</strong> — Describe the task or feature you're working with</li>
<li><strong>What's happening instead</strong> — The error message or unexpected behavior you're seeing</li>
<li><strong>Steps to reproduce</strong> — What did you click/do before the issue appeared?</li>
<li><strong>Browser and OS</strong> — e.g., "Chrome 121 on macOS 14"</li>
<li><strong>Screenshots or screen recording</strong> — A picture is worth a thousand words for UI bugs</li>
</ul>

<h2>Self-Service First Steps</h2>
<p>Before contacting support, try these quick fixes for common issues:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Clear your browser cache</strong> — Many display issues resolve with a hard refresh (<kbd>Ctrl+Shift+R</kbd> / <kbd>Cmd+Shift+R</kbd>)</li>
<li><strong>Try incognito/private mode</strong> — This rules out browser extension conflicts</li>
<li><strong>Try a different browser</strong> — Chrome, Firefox, and Edge are all fully supported</li>
<li><strong>Check the Common Issues &amp; Solutions article</strong> — Covers the most frequent problems</li>
</ol>

<h2>Feature Requests and Bug Reports</h2>
<p>To request a new feature or report a confirmed bug, email <strong>support@finaldoc.io</strong> with the subject "Feature Request: [feature]" or "Bug Report: [issue]". Your feedback directly influences our roadmap.</p>

<h2>Privacy and Security Concerns</h2>
<p>For privacy-related inquiries (GDPR, data deletion requests, data processing agreements) or security vulnerability reports, email <strong>privacy@finaldoc.io</strong>. Security vulnerabilities should be reported responsibly — do not post them publicly.</p>
