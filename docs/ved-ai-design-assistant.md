<h1>Ved AI Design Assistant</h1>
<p>The Ved AI Design Assistant helps you customize and improve the visual appearance of your public knowledge base using natural language instructions. Instead of manually adjusting colors, fonts, and layout settings in the Portal Builder, you describe what you want and the AI applies the changes for you.</p>

<h2>Opening the Design Assistant</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings → Appearance → Design Studio</strong></li>
<li>Click the <strong>AI Design Assistant</strong> button (magic wand icon)</li>
</ol>
<p>Or from the Portal Builder, click the <strong>Ask AI</strong> button in the top-right.</p>

<h2>How It Works</h2>
<p>The Design Assistant understands natural language requests about your KB's visual design. You type what you want, and it translates that into changes to your Portal Builder settings.</p>

<h2>Example Prompts</h2>
<ul>
<li>"Make the header background dark blue with white text"</li>
<li>"Use our brand colors — primary is #7C3AED, secondary is #E5E7EB"</li>
<li>"Make the search bar larger and more prominent on the homepage"</li>
<li>"Add a hero banner with a gradient background"</li>
<li>"Make the article cards have rounded corners and a subtle shadow"</li>
<li>"Increase the font size for better readability"</li>
<li>"Create a clean, minimal design with lots of white space"</li>
</ul>

<h2>Previewing Changes</h2>
<p>After the AI applies changes, a preview of your KB appears. Compare the before and after:</p>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Preview Changes</strong> to see the updated KB design</li>
<li>If you like the changes, click <strong>Apply</strong> to save them</li>
<li>If you don't like them, click <strong>Undo</strong> to revert</li>
<li>Or type a new instruction to refine further (e.g., "The purple is too dark, make it lighter")</li>
</ol>

<h2>What the Design Assistant Can Change</h2>
<ul>
<li>Color scheme (primary, secondary, background, text, border colors)</li>
<li>Typography (font family, font sizes, heading styles)</li>
<li>Header configuration (background, height, logo position)</li>
<li>Homepage sections (hero banner, category cards, featured articles)</li>
<li>Article display settings (sidebar, TOC position, spacing)</li>
<li>Button styles (rounded, square, outlined, filled)</li>
</ul>

<h2>What It Cannot Change</h2>
<p>The Design Assistant works within FinalDoc's Portal Builder settings. It cannot:</p>
<ul>
<li>Make changes that require custom CSS (very specific pixel-level adjustments)</li>
<li>Create entirely new layout sections that aren't in the Portal Builder</li>
<li>Change article content or structure</li>
</ul>
<p>For changes beyond Portal Builder capabilities, use Settings → Appearance → Custom CSS.</p>

<h2>Tips</h2>
<ul>
<li>Be specific about colors — say "dark navy blue (#1e3a5f)" rather than just "dark blue"</li>
<li>Describe the feel, not just technical changes — "professional and corporate" or "friendly and approachable" gives the AI useful context</li>
<li>You can iterate — make a change, preview it, then refine with another prompt</li>
</ul>
