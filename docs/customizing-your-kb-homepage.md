<h1>Customizing Your KB Homepage</h1>
<p>Your knowledge base homepage is the first thing readers see. A well-configured homepage helps them find what they need quickly and makes a great first impression. FinalDoc gives you full control over the homepage layout and content through the Portal Builder.</p>

<h2>Opening Homepage Settings</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings → Appearance → Portal Builder</strong></li>
<li>Scroll to the <strong>Homepage</strong> section</li>
</ol>

<h2>Homepage Sections</h2>
<p>Your homepage is made of sections you can enable, disable, and reorder by dragging:</p>

<h3>Hero Banner</h3>
<p>The top section with a large heading and search bar. Configure:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Title</strong>: "How can we help?" or "Welcome to [Product Name] Help"</li>
<li><strong>Subtitle</strong>: A brief tagline or description</li>
<li><strong>Background</strong>: Solid color (uses your primary brand color by default), gradient, or image</li>
<li><strong>Show search bar</strong>: Recommended ON — most readers come to search</li>
</ul>

<h3>Category Cards</h3>
<p>A grid of cards, one per category, showing the category icon/name and article count. Readers click to jump into a category. This helps readers who are browsing rather than searching.</p>
<ul>
<li>Number of columns: 2, 3, or 4</li>
<li>Show/hide article counts</li>
<li>Show/hide category descriptions</li>
</ul>

<h3>Featured Articles</h3>
<p>Manually select articles to highlight on the homepage. Use for your most important or most-read content — "Getting Started", "Installation Guide", "FAQ", etc.</p>
<ol>
<li>Enable the Featured Articles section</li>
<li>Click <strong>Select Articles</strong></li>
<li>Choose up to 6 articles</li>
<li>Drag to reorder them</li>
</ol>

<h3>Popular Articles</h3>
<p>Auto-populated list of your most-viewed articles, based on actual reader data. No manual curation needed — FinalDoc picks the top articles for you.</p>

<h3>Recent Articles</h3>
<p>Shows the most recently published or updated articles. Useful for KBs that publish frequently and want readers to see new content.</p>

<h2>Custom HTML/CSS for the Homepage</h2>
<p>For advanced customization beyond the visual settings, add custom HTML blocks to the homepage via <strong>Settings → Appearance → Custom CSS &amp; JavaScript</strong>. You can add custom content sections, promotional banners, or embedded content.</p>

<h2>Preview Before Saving</h2>
<p>All changes show in the live preview panel on the right. Check the preview in both desktop and mobile layouts (toggle with the device icons at the top of the preview). When happy, click <strong>Save</strong>.</p>
