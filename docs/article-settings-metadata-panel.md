<h1>Article Settings &amp; Metadata Panel</h1>
<p>Every article in FinalDoc has a metadata panel — the Inspector panel on the right side of the editor — where you configure the article's properties beyond its content. This includes SEO settings, publishing options, author, tags, and reader experience settings.</p>

<h2>Opening the Inspector Panel</h2>
<p>The Inspector panel is visible on the right side of the Documentation editor whenever you have an article open. If you don't see it:</p>
<ol>
<li>Click the <strong>inspector icon</strong> (three horizontal lines or a sidebar icon) in the editor toolbar</li>
<li>Or press <strong>Ctrl+Shift+I</strong> (Cmd+Shift+I on Mac) to toggle it</li>
</ol>

<h2>General Settings</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Title</strong> — The article's displayed title (can also be edited inline in the article)</li>
<li><strong>Slug</strong> — The URL path for this article (auto-generated from title; editable). Keep it short and use hyphens, not spaces</li>
<li><strong>Draft Writer</strong> — The team member shown as the article's author. Defaults to who created it; can be changed to any team member</li>
<li><strong>Status</strong> — Draft, Published, or Archived. Change here to publish or unpublish</li>
</ul>

<h2>SEO Settings</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Description</strong> — The meta description for this article (shown in search engine results). 130–160 characters recommended</li>
<li><strong>Featured Image</strong> — The article's thumbnail image, used in article listings, social media previews (Open Graph), and category cards</li>
<li><strong>Canonical URL</strong> — If this article exists on another URL and you want search engines to treat that as the primary, enter the canonical URL here. Leave blank for normal articles</li>
</ul>

<h2>Organization</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Category</strong> — Which folder this article belongs to. Drag to move in the sidebar, or change here</li>
<li><strong>Tags</strong> — Keywords/labels applied to this article. Used for related article suggestions and tag-based filtering on the KB</li>
<li><strong>Order</strong> — Manual sort position within the category (drag in the sidebar to reorder is easier)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Reader Experience</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Table of Contents</strong> — Whether to show the auto-generated TOC panel for this article (overrides the global setting for this article only)</li>
<li><strong>Feedback</strong> — Enable or disable the thumbs up/down feedback widget for this specific article</li>
<li><strong>Reactions</strong> — Enable or disable emoji reactions for this article</li>
<li><strong>Comments</strong> — Allow or disallow reader comments on this article (if reader comments are enabled globally)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Version History</h2>
<p>At the bottom of the Inspector, the <strong>Version History</strong> section shows a list of recent saves with timestamps and author names. Click any version to preview or restore it.</p>

<h2>Change Note</h2>
<p>Before saving an article, enter a brief description of your changes in the <strong>Change Note</strong> field. This note appears in the version history and optionally in the public Article Changelog. Example: "Updated screenshots for v3 UI".</p>
