<h1>Bulk Actions and Article Management</h1>
<p>When you need to manage many articles at once — publish a batch of drafts, move articles between categories, or clean up outdated content — FinalDoc's bulk actions save you from doing it one by one.</p>

<h2>Enabling Bulk Select Mode</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Documentation</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
<li>In the article list panel, look for a <strong>checkbox icon</strong> or <strong>Bulk Select</strong> button near the top</li>
<li>Click it to enter bulk select mode — checkboxes appear next to every article</li>
<li>Click the checkboxes for the articles you want to act on</li>
</ol>
<p>You can also click the <strong>Select All</strong> checkbox at the top to select every article in the current view at once.</p>

<h2>Available Bulk Actions</h2>
<p>Once one or more articles are selected, a bulk action toolbar appears:</p>
<table>
<thead><tr><th>Action</th><th>What It Does</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td><strong>Publish All</strong></td><td>Changes all selected draft articles to Published status — they go live immediately</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Set to Draft</strong></td><td>Unpublishes all selected published articles — removes them from the public KB without deleting them</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Move to Category</strong></td><td>Moves all selected articles to a different category. A dropdown lets you choose the target category</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Add Tags</strong></td><td>Applies one or more tags to all selected articles simultaneously</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Export</strong></td><td>Downloads all selected articles as a ZIP file (Markdown format by default)</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Delete</strong></td><td>Moves all selected articles to the Recycle Bin (recoverable within 30 days)</td></tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<h2>Filtering Articles</h2>
<p>Before running bulk actions, use the filter controls to narrow down which articles are shown:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>By status</strong> — Show only Drafts, Published, In Review, or Archived articles</li>
<li><strong>By category</strong> — Show articles from a specific category</li>
<li><strong>By tag</strong> — Show articles with a specific tag</li>
<li><strong>By author</strong> — Show articles written by a specific team member</li>
<li><strong>Search</strong> — Type a keyword to filter by article title</li>
</ul>

<h2>Recycle Bin</h2>
<p>Deleted articles are not gone forever. They are moved to the <strong>Recycle Bin</strong> where they sit for 30 days before being permanently erased.</p>
<p>To access the Recycle Bin:</p>
<ol>
<li>In Documentation view, look for the <strong>trash icon</strong> or a "Recycle Bin" link at the bottom of the article panel</li>
<li>Click it to see all recently deleted articles</li>
<li>Click <strong>Restore</strong> next to any article to bring it back (it returns to Draft status)</li>
<li>Click <strong>Delete Permanently</strong> to erase it for good before the 30-day window</li>
</ol>

<h2>Article Search (Admin)</h2>
<p>To find a specific article quickly inside the admin panel:</p>
<ul>
<li>Use the <strong>search bar</strong> at the top of the article tree to filter by title as you type</li>
<li>This is a local filter — it only searches articles already loaded in the sidebar</li>
<li>For a full-text search across all article content, use the global search bar in the top navigation</li>
</ul>

<h2>Duplicate an Article</h2>
<p>To create a copy of an article (useful for templating or translating existing content):</p>
<ol>
<li>Right-click the article in the sidebar</li>
<li>Choose <strong>Duplicate</strong></li>
<li>A copy is created with "(Copy)" appended to the title, in the same category, in Draft status</li>
<li>Open it, rename it, and edit the content</li>
</ol>
