<h1>PDF Export</h1>
<p>FinalDoc's PDF Export tool lets you generate professional, branded PDF documents from your articles. Export a single article, an entire category, or your whole knowledge base. Great for creating offline documentation, distributing printed manuals, or archiving snapshots of your docs at a point in time.</p>

<h2>Opening PDF Export</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Toolbox</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
<li>Under <strong>Import and Export</strong>, click <strong>PDF Export</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>Choosing What to Export</h2>
<p>You can export:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Selected articles</strong> — Check the checkboxes next to any articles you want. You can pick articles from multiple categories</li>
<li><strong>Entire category</strong> — Click the checkbox next to a category to select all articles in it</li>
<li><strong>All articles</strong> — Use "Select All" to export your entire knowledge base as one PDF</li>
</ul>

<h2>PDF Settings</h2>
<p>Before exporting, configure the PDF options:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Template</strong> — Choose a PDF layout template: Classic, Modern, Minimal, or your custom template</li>
<li><strong>Cover page</strong> — Include a branded cover page with your logo, title, and date</li>
<li><strong>Table of contents</strong> — Include an auto-generated clickable table of contents at the start</li>
<li><strong>Header and footer</strong> — Add your company name, article title, or page numbers to every page</li>
<li><strong>Include change notes</strong> — Add a Changelog section at the end of each article (from Version History)</li>
<li><strong>Page size</strong> — A4 (default) or US Letter</li>
<li><strong>Font size</strong> — Adjust for readability</li>
</ul>

<h2>Branding the PDF</h2>
<p>Your PDF uses the branding configured in your project:</p>
<ul>
<li>Your project logo appears on the cover page and header</li>
<li>Your brand primary color is used for headings and links</li>
<li>Your project name appears in the document title</li>
</ul>
<p>To customize branding further, go to <strong>Settings → Appearance → Design Studio</strong> to update your logo and colors — these flow through to PDF exports.</p>

<h2>Generating the PDF</h2>
<ol>
<li>Select articles, configure settings</li>
<li>Click <strong>Generate PDF</strong></li>
<li>The PDF is generated in the background (large exports may take a minute or two)</li>
<li>When ready, a download link appears. Click it to download the PDF to your computer</li>
<li>Previous exports are listed so you can re-download without regenerating</li>
</ol>

<h2>Single Article PDF Export</h2>
<p>For a quick single-article export, you can also trigger a PDF from directly inside the article editor:</p>
<ol>
<li>Open the article</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Export</strong> button (usually a download icon) in the top toolbar</li>
<li>Choose <strong>Export as PDF</strong></li>
<li>The PDF downloads immediately</li>
</ol>

<h2>Images in PDF</h2>
<p>All images in your articles — including uploaded images, screenshots, and media from Drive — are automatically included in the generated PDF. Images are rendered at their original dimensions (max-width 100%) with rounded corners.</p>

<h2>Limitations</h2>
<ul>
<li>Interactive elements (embedded videos, interactive API explorer) are shown as static screenshots or links in the PDF</li>
<li>Very large exports (500+ articles) may take several minutes to generate</li>
<li>Code blocks preserve syntax highlighting in PDF output</li>
</ul>
