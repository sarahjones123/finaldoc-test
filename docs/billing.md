<h1>Billing</h1>
<p>The Billing section is where you view your current plan, monitor usage, upgrade or downgrade, update your payment method, and download invoices. Only the project OWNER can access Billing — Admins and Editors cannot see this section.</p>

<h2>Opening Billing</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings → Billing</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>Current Plan Overview</h2>
<p>At the top of the Billing page:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Plan name</strong> — Starter, Business, or Enterprise</li>
<li><strong>Billing cycle</strong> — Monthly or Annual</li>
<li><strong>Renewal date</strong> — When your next payment is due (or when your annual plan ends)</li>
<li><strong>Current period usage</strong> — Articles published, AI tokens used, storage used, readers</li>
</ul>

<h2>Plan Limits</h2>
<p>A visual dashboard shows your usage vs. plan limits for key resources:</p>
<ul>
<li>Published articles</li>
<li>Readers (registered reader accounts)</li>
<li>Drive storage (GB used / total)</li>
<li>AI tokens (used this month / monthly allowance)</li>
<li>Workspaces</li>
</ul>
<p>When you approach a limit, a warning indicator appears. When you hit a limit, the affected feature is restricted until you upgrade or the limit resets.</p>

<h2>Upgrading Your Plan</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Upgrade Plan</strong></li>
<li>The plan comparison page shows Starter, Business, and Enterprise</li>
<li>Select a plan and billing cycle (monthly or annual — annual plans are discounted)</li>
<li>Enter payment details</li>
<li>Click <strong>Subscribe</strong></li>
<li>Your account upgrades immediately — new limits take effect right away</li>
</ol>

<h2>Payment Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Update Payment Method</strong></li>
<li>Enter your new credit card details</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>FinalDoc processes payments via Stripe. Card details are handled entirely by Stripe — FinalDoc never stores raw card numbers.</p>

<h2>Invoices</h2>
<p>Scroll to the <strong>Invoice History</strong> section to see all past payments. Click any invoice row to download a PDF of the invoice for accounting purposes.</p>

<h2>Cancelling Your Subscription</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Cancel Plan</strong> at the bottom of the Billing page</li>
<li>Your subscription cancels at the end of the current billing period</li>
<li>You retain full access until the period ends</li>
<li>After cancellation, your account moves to a read-only free tier (articles and data are preserved but no new articles can be published)</li>
</ol>
<p>To permanently delete your account and all data, contact support@finaldoc.io after cancelling.</p>
