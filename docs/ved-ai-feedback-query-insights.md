<h1>Ved AI Feedback &amp; Query Insights</h1>
<p>The <strong>Ved AI</strong> tab in the Feedback section shows how readers interact with the AI chatbot on your knowledge base. Use these insights to improve both your documentation and the AI&rsquo;s effectiveness.</p>

<h2>Enabling AI Chatbot Feedback</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings &rarr; AI (Ved AI) &rarr; Enable &amp; Configure</strong></li>
<li>Toggle <strong>Reader Assistant</strong> ON</li>
</ol>
<p>When enabled, readers can chat with the AI assistant on your public KB. Each AI response includes a "Was this helpful?" prompt.</p>

<h2>Two Sub-tabs</h2>

<h3>Feedback Tab</h3>
<p>Shows reader ratings on AI chatbot responses:</p>
<ul>
<li>Each entry shows the question the reader asked, the AI&rsquo;s response, and whether the reader rated it helpful or not helpful</li>
<li>Use negative ratings to identify where the AI gives poor answers &mdash; usually a sign that the underlying article needs improvement</li>
</ul>

<h3>Unanswered Tab</h3>
<p>Shows questions the AI could not answer:</p>
<ul>
<li>These are content gaps &mdash; topics your readers are looking for but your KB doesn&rsquo;t cover</li>
<li>Use these to prioritize new articles. You can also use <strong>Analytics &rarr; Search Analytics &rarr; Content Gaps</strong> to auto-generate draft articles from unanswered queries</li>
</ul>

<h2>Stats Cards</h2>
<p>The header shows three cards:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Total</strong> &mdash; Total AI feedback items</li>
<li><strong>Positive %</strong> &mdash; Percentage of helpful ratings</li>
<li><strong>Negative %</strong> &mdash; Percentage of not-helpful ratings</li>
</ul>

<h2>Improving AI Quality</h2>
<p>When you see negative AI feedback:</p>
<ol>
<li>Read the question and the AI&rsquo;s response</li>
<li>If the AI gave a wrong answer &mdash; the source article likely needs updating</li>
<li>If the AI couldn&rsquo;t find an answer &mdash; create a new article covering that topic</li>
<li>After updating articles, re-embed them: go to <strong>Settings &rarr; AI &rarr; Sources</strong> and click <strong>Rebuild Embeddings</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>The AI&rsquo;s quality improves as your documentation becomes more comprehensive and accurate.</p>
