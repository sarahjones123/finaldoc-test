<h1>Ticket Deflection &amp; Impact Metrics</h1>
<p>Ticket Deflection metrics show how effectively your knowledge base is preventing support tickets — when a reader finds the answer in your KB instead of submitting a ticket, that's a deflected ticket. FinalDoc estimates deflection rates and tracks the impact of your documentation work over time.</p>

<h2>Opening Deflection Metrics</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Ticket Analysis</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Impact Metrics</strong> tab</li>
</ol>

<h2>How Deflection Is Estimated</h2>
<p>FinalDoc estimates deflection using a combination of signals:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Widget searches that didn't lead to a contact form submission</strong> — Reader searched, found an article, did not submit a ticket. Likely self-served</li>
<li><strong>KB searches before ticket submission</strong> — If a reader visited the KB and then submitted a ticket within 30 minutes, that may indicate a search-but-didn't-find situation</li>
<li><strong>Ticket topic overlap with KB content</strong> — Topics that decreased in ticket volume after new KB articles were published</li>
</ul>
<p>These are estimates — precise deflection counting requires integration with your helpdesk's ticket source and KB search data together.</p>

<h2>Deflection Metrics Shown</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Estimated deflection rate</strong> — Percentage of potential tickets estimated to have been self-served via the KB</li>
<li><strong>Estimated tickets deflected (monthly)</strong> — How many tickets you avoided per month</li>
<li><strong>Estimated cost savings</strong> — Based on your configured average ticket handling cost (set in Impact Metrics settings)</li>
<li><strong>KB self-serve attempts</strong> — Total searches in your KB that appear to have successfully resolved a reader's question</li>
</ul>

<h2>Before and After Comparison</h2>
<p>When you create or update articles in response to Ticket Analysis recommendations, FinalDoc tracks whether those topics show fewer tickets in the following weeks. The <strong>Impact of Changes</strong> section shows:</p>
<ul>
<li>Articles created/updated from recommendations</li>
<li>Topic ticket volume before the article was published</li>
<li>Topic ticket volume after the article was published</li>
<li>Estimated tickets deflected by that article</li>
</ul>

<h2>Configuring Cost Per Ticket</h2>
<p>To see cost savings estimates:</p>
<ol>
<li>In Impact Metrics, click <strong>Settings</strong></li>
<li>Enter your <strong>average cost per support ticket</strong> (e.g., $15)</li>
<li>FinalDoc multiplies estimated deflected tickets by this cost to show dollar savings</li>
</ol>
<p>Common estimates: $8–12 for email support tickets, $15–25 for tickets requiring agent research.</p>

<h2>Sharing Impact Reports</h2>
<p>Click <strong>Export Report</strong> to download a PDF or CSV of the deflection metrics. Useful for presenting documentation ROI to management or demonstrating the value of your KB investment.</p>
