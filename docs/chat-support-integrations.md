<h1>Chat &amp; Support Integrations</h1>
<p>The Chat &amp; Support tab combines live chat tools, help desk integrations, team collaboration, and the AI Agent into one place. When readers can't find what they need in the docs, they can escalate to live support or get AI-powered answers without leaving the page.</p>

<h2>Opening Chat &amp; Support</h2>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
<li>Under <strong>Integrations</strong>, click <strong>Chat &amp; Support</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>AI Agent (OpenClaw)</h2>
<p>At the top of the Chat &amp; Support page you'll find the AI Agent card. OpenClaw deploys an AI-powered chatbot that connects your knowledge base to WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, Microsoft Teams, and web chat. Readers can search articles and get AI-generated answers from any messaging platform.</p>
<p>Click <strong>Configure</strong> to open the Agent Settings panel. See <a href="/article/ai-agent-openclaw">AI Agent (OpenClaw)</a> for complete setup instructions.</p>

<h2>Live Chat Platforms</h2>
<p>These tools embed a chat widget on your KB so readers can talk to a human agent:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Intercom</strong> — Embed Intercom Messenger on your KB. Readers can chat, search articles, or submit tickets without leaving the page</li>
<li><strong>Crisp</strong> — Real-time chat with customers directly from your KB. Includes chatbot support</li>
<li><strong>LiveChat</strong> — Professional live chat with queuing and team routing</li>
<li><strong>Freshchat</strong> — Freshworks' messaging platform embedded in your KB</li>
<li><strong>Chatra</strong> — Simple live chat with offline messaging support</li>
<li><strong>Gorgias</strong> — E-commerce-focused helpdesk with multi-channel support</li>
<li><strong>Kommunicate</strong> — AI chatbot with human handoff for support escalation</li>
<li><strong>Botco</strong> — Conversational AI chatbot for customer support</li>
<li><strong>Quriobot</strong> — No-code chatbot builder for lead capture and support</li>
<li><strong>Sunshine Conversations (Smooch)</strong> — Zendesk's messaging platform for multi-channel conversations</li>
</ul>

<h2>Help Desks</h2>
<p>Connect your help desk so support agents can search and share KB articles directly from tickets:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Freshdesk</strong> — Search and send articles in customer conversations</li>
<li><strong>Freshservice</strong> — Contextual articles in IT service tickets</li>
<li><strong>Zendesk</strong> — Search, share, and create KB articles from Zendesk tickets</li>
<li><strong>Intercom</strong> — Suggest and share articles from Intercom conversations</li>
<li><strong>Salesforce</strong> — Create, search, and send articles within Salesforce (enterprise plan)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Team Collaboration</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Slack</strong> — <code>/finaldoc</code> commands to search your KB and receive publish notifications</li>
<li><strong>Microsoft Teams</strong> — <code>@FinalDoc</code> mentions for AI-powered Q&amp;A in Teams channels. See <a href="/article/microsoft-teams-integration">Microsoft Teams Integration</a></li>
<li><strong>Drift</strong> — Suggest docs to customers automatically in Drift conversations</li>
</ul>

<h2>Feedback &amp; Comments</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Disqus</strong> — Threaded comment system at the bottom of articles</li>
<li><strong>Commento</strong> — Privacy-focused, ad-free comment system</li>
<li><strong>Doorbell</strong> — In-app feedback collection</li>
</ul>

<h2>Setting Up a Chat Integration</h2>
<ol>
<li>Find the tool you want and click <strong>Connect</strong></li>
<li>Enter the required credentials (usually an API key, widget ID, or tracking code)</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>The chat widget will appear on your public KB immediately. Connected integrations show a green "Active" badge.</p>
