<h1>Ved AI Writer Assistant</h1>
<p>The Ved AI Writer Assistant is your AI writing partner inside the Documentation editor. It helps you write, improve, expand, summarize, and rephrase content — without leaving the article you are working on. Think of it as having a writing expert always on call.</p>

<h2>Opening the Writer Assistant</h2>
<p>There are two ways to access the Writer Assistant:</p>
<ul>
<li>Click the <strong>Ved AI</strong> button in the top navigation bar (star icon) — opens the assistant in a side panel</li>
<li>Select text in the editor and click the AI icon that appears in the formatting popup — for selection-specific operations</li>
</ul>

<h2>What the Writer Assistant Can Do</h2>
<h3>Writing Assistance</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Continue writing</strong> — Place your cursor and tell the AI "continue from here" — it picks up your train of thought and keeps going</li>
<li><strong>Write a section</strong> — Tell the AI "write a troubleshooting section for common installation errors" — it generates structured content</li>
<li><strong>Explain a concept</strong> — "Write a plain-language explanation of OAuth 2.0 for non-technical readers"</li>
</ul>

<h3>Editing and Improving</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Improve writing</strong> — Select a section and ask the AI to improve readability, clarity, or conciseness</li>
<li><strong>Fix grammar and spelling</strong> — Select text → "Fix grammar" — corrects without changing meaning</li>
<li><strong>Simplify</strong> — "Make this easier to understand" — rewrites in simpler language</li>
<li><strong>Formalize</strong> — "Make this more professional and formal"</li>
</ul>

<h3>Content Transformation</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Summarize</strong> — Select a long section → "Summarize this in 2 sentences" — produces a concise summary</li>
<li><strong>Expand</strong> — Select a brief paragraph → "Expand this with more detail and examples"</li>
<li><strong>Convert to bullet list</strong> — Select a paragraph → "Convert to a bullet list"</li>
<li><strong>Convert to numbered steps</strong> — Select a paragraph → "Turn this into step-by-step instructions"</li>
</ul>

<h3>SEO and Meta</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Generate description</strong> — "Write an SEO description for this article" — generates a 155-character summary</li>
<li><strong>Suggest title</strong> — "Suggest five SEO-friendly title options for this article"</li>
<li><strong>Suggest tags</strong> — "What tags should this article have?"</li>
</ul>

<h3>AI Chat Mode</h3>
<p>In the side panel, you can have a free-form conversation with the AI. Ask it anything about your article, ask for suggestions, or use it as a research assistant. The AI has access to the full content of the article you are currently editing.</p>

<h2>Style Guide Integration</h2>
<p>The Writer Assistant follows your configured style guide from <strong>Settings → AI (Ved AI) → AI Style Guide &amp; Brand Voice</strong>. If you define rules like "always use active voice", "avoid jargon", or "refer to users as 'you' not 'the user'", the AI applies these automatically to all generated content.</p>

<h2>Inserting AI-Generated Content</h2>
<p>When the AI generates content:</p>
<ul>
<li>A preview appears in the AI panel</li>
<li>Click <strong>Insert</strong> to add it at the cursor position</li>
<li>Click <strong>Replace</strong> to replace the selected text (if text was selected)</li>
<li>Click <strong>Regenerate</strong> if you want a different version</li>
<li>Click <strong>Discard</strong> to dismiss the suggestion without making any changes</li>
</ul>
