<p>Getting started with FinalDoc is quick and straightforward. This guide walks you through the complete process of creating your account, accessing your project, and preparing your knowledge base for use.</p>
<h2>Step 1: Sign up</h2>
<p>Follow these steps to create your FinalDoc account.</p>
<ol>
<li><p>Open your browser and navigate to: <strong>app.finaldoc.io</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li><p>On the login page, click <strong>Create one</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<img src="/api/public/media/id_mn5oivzw_3ky1rxu5y" alt="" width="400" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />
    
<ol start="3">
<li><p>Fill in the registration form.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Company name</strong> — this becomes your account and project name (for example, <em>Acme Corp</em>).</li>
<li><strong>Your name</strong> — your full name as it will appear to your team.</li>
<li><strong>Email address</strong> — use a valid work email. This will be your login credential.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Create account</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Check your email inbox for a verification email.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Click the verification link to set your password and activate your account.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Log in using your email and newly created password.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>If you do not receive the verification email, check your spam or junk folder. The email is sent from <strong><a href="mailto:hello@finaldoc.io">hello@finaldoc.io</a></strong>. If it does not arrive within a few minutes, try signing up again or contact support.</p>
<hr>
<h2>Step 2: Review your project</h2>
<p>Once you log in for the first time, FinalDoc automatically creates a default project for you. No manual setup is required.</p>
<ul>
<li>The project acts as the <strong>top-level container</strong> for your knowledge base.</li>
<li>All articles, categories, settings, and users are managed within this project.</li>
<li>A default workspace named <strong>v1</strong> is also created automatically.</li>
<li>This workspace is where you will create and manage your documentation.</li>
</ul>
<p>You can rename your project at any time in <strong>Settings → General → Project info</strong>.</p>
<hr>
<h2>Step 3: Access your public URL</h2>
<p>Each FinalDoc project includes a default public URL that lets you share your knowledge base instantly.</p>
<p><code>https://your-project-name.finaldoc.io</code></p>
<ul>
<li>This URL is automatically generated based on your project name.</li>
<li>It can be shared with readers immediately after account creation.</li>
<li>No additional configuration is required to make your content accessible.</li>
</ul>
<p>If needed, you can configure a custom domain later from <strong>Settings → Domain &amp; SEO → Custom domain</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Example</strong>: <code>docs.yourcompany.com</code></p>
<hr>
<h2>Step 4: Invite your team (optional)</h2>
<p>If you plan to collaborate with others, you can invite team members to your project.</p>
<ol>
<li><p>Navigate to <strong>Settings</strong> from the left sidebar.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Go to <strong>Security &amp; Access → Team Management</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Invite Member</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Enter the team member’s email address.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Select an appropriate role.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Admin</strong> — full access to settings and content (cannot delete the account).</li>
<li><strong>Editor</strong> — can create, edit, and publish articles.</li>
<li><strong>Draft Writer</strong> — can create and edit content but requires approval to publish.</li>
<li><strong>Viewer</strong> — read-only access within the admin panel.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><p>Click <strong>Send Invite</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The invited user will receive an email with instructions to join your workspace.</p>
<p>For more details, refer to the <strong>Roles and permissions guide</strong>.</p>
<hr>
<h2>What to do next</h2>
<p>Your account is now ready. To continue setting up your knowledge base, follow these recommended steps.</p>
<ol>
<li>Follow the <strong>Quick start guide</strong> to create your first category and article.</li>
<li>Read <strong>Understanding the interface</strong> to familiarise yourself with the platform layout.</li>
<li>Go to <strong>Design studio</strong> in Settings to add your brand logo, configure colours and themes, and customise the look and feel of your knowledge base.</li>
</ol>
