<h1>Feedback Management</h1>
<p>The Feedback section is your central hub for all reader feedback. Click <strong>Feedback</strong> in the left sidebar to open it. The page has three tabs covering different feedback sources.</p>

<h2>Three Feedback Tabs</h2>
<table>
<thead><tr><th>Tab</th><th>What It Shows</th><th>How Readers Submit</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td><strong>Article Feedback</strong></td><td>Thumbs up/down ratings and optional comments</td><td>Readers click "Yes" or "No" on the "Helpful?" widget at the bottom of articles</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Ved AI</strong></td><td>Feedback on AI chatbot responses and unanswered questions</td><td>Readers rate chatbot answers as helpful/not helpful in the KB chatbot</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Portal Issues</strong></td><td>Issue reports — wrong info, missing steps, broken links</td><td>Readers click "Report Issue" &rarr; "Flag" &rarr; select issue type &rarr; submit</td></tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<h2>Enabling Feedback Collection</h2>
<table>
<thead><tr><th>Feedback Type</th><th>How to Enable</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td><strong>Article feedback</strong></td><td>Settings &rarr; Appearance &rarr; Article Display &rarr; expand <strong>Article bottom</strong> &rarr; toggle <strong>Show feedback form</strong> ON</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>AI chatbot feedback</strong></td><td>Settings &rarr; AI (Ved AI) &rarr; Enable &amp; Configure &rarr; toggle <strong>Reader Assistant</strong> ON</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Portal issues</strong></td><td>Enabled automatically when the article feedback form is active</td></tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<h2>Header Stats</h2>
<p>At the top of each tab you see summary cards:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Article Feedback</strong> &mdash; Total count, positive %, negative %, resolved count</li>
<li><strong>Ved AI</strong> &mdash; Total, positive %, negative %</li>
<li><strong>Portal Issues</strong> &mdash; Missing Steps count, Broken Links count, Wrong Info count</li>
</ul>

<h2>Sidebar Quick Stats</h2>
<p>The left sidebar shows at-a-glance numbers:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Total Feedback</strong> &mdash; All feedback items across types</li>
<li><strong>Unanswered</strong> &mdash; Items not yet resolved (red badge)</li>
<li><strong>Resolved</strong> &mdash; Items marked as resolved</li>
</ul>

<h2>Analytics Sections (Article Feedback tab)</h2>
<p>Three collapsible sections appear above the feedback list:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>14-Day Sentiment Trend</strong> &mdash; Area chart showing positive (green) vs negative (red) feedback over the last 14 days</li>
<li><strong>AI Insights</strong> &mdash; Priority articles with most unresolved negative feedback, trending issues (7-day spikes), common themes from comments, and sentiment heatmap (color-coded articles by % negative)</li>
<li><strong>Your Performance</strong> &mdash; Resolution streak (consecutive days at 100%), total resolved count, average response time, and 6 unlockable achievements</li>
</ul>

<h2>Searching &amp; Filtering</h2>
<p>Use the controls above the feedback list:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Search</strong> &mdash; Type to filter by article title or comment text (keyboard shortcut: <code>/</code>)</li>
<li><strong>Rating filter</strong> &mdash; All ratings, Positive only, Negative only</li>
<li><strong>Status filter</strong> &mdash; All statuses, New, In Review, Assigned, Resolved, Archived</li>
<li><strong>Date filter</strong> &mdash; All time, Last 7 days, Last 30 days, Last 90 days</li>
</ul>

<h2>Exporting Feedback</h2>
<p>Click the <strong>Export</strong> dropdown to download feedback as:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>JSON</strong> &mdash; Full data with all fields</li>
<li><strong>CSV</strong> &mdash; Spreadsheet-compatible format</li>
</ul>
